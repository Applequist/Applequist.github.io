## What are you talking about ? 

I am talking about the [mermaid](https://mermaid.js.org), the javascript diagramming framework!

Here is an example:

```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    Alice->>John: Hello John, how are you?
    John-->>Alice: Great!
    Alice-)John: See you later!
```
